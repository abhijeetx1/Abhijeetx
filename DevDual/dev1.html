About Us

Welcome to the 59th Foundation Day Celebration of CSJM UNIVERSITY We are excited to commemorate this special occasion with various events and activities.

Our institution was founded on with the vision of 59th Foundation Over the years, we have been dedicated. One of the highlights of the Foundation Day celebration is the convocation ceremony, where graduating students are conferred with degrees, marking the culmination of their academic journey at the university. This ceremony not only acknowledges the hard work and perseverance of the students but also serves as a testament to the university's commitment to nurturing future leaders and professionals. In addition to the convocation ceremony, CSJM University Foundation Day features academic symposiums, conferences, and seminars that provide a platform for scholars, researchers, and academicians to exchange ideas, share insights, and engage in meaningful discourse on various topics of importance. These intellectual gatherings not only foster academic collaboration but also contribute to the advancement of knowledge in diverse fields. The celebration also includes cultural programs, exhibitions, and competitions that showcase the artistic and creative talents of the students. From music and dance performances to art exhibitions and literary competitions, these events add a vibrant and festive atmosphere to the Foundation Day celebrations, bringing together students, faculty, staff, alumni, and the local community. Moreover, CSJM University Foundation Day is an occasion to honor and recognize individuals who have made significant contributions to the university's growth and development. Whether it be distinguished alumni, eminent faculty members, or philanthropic supporters, their achievements and dedication are celebrated as an integral part of the university's legacy. Beyond the festivities and accolades, CSJM University Foundation Day holds a deeper significance – it serves as a reaffirmation of the university's commitment to its core values of excellence, integrity, and inclusivity. It is a reminder of the responsibility entrusted upon the institution to empower individuals, transform lives, and contribute to the betterment of society. As CSJM University continues its journey of academic excellence and societal impact, each Foundation Day serves as a milestone, a moment to pause, reflect, and renew the commitment to its mission of education, research, and service. It is a celebration of the past, a recognition of the present, and a promise for the future – a testament to the enduring legacy of CSJM University and its profound impact on generations to come.
